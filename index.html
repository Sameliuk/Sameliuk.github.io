My favourite city

Prague is one of the most beautiful cities I have ever visited. It is famous for its stunning medieval architecture, narrow cobblestone streets, and the magical atmosphere of the Old Town. The Charles Bridge, with its historic statues, offers a wonderful view of the Vltava River, while Prague Castle impresses with its size and history. Walking through the city, you can feel a mix of old traditions and modern life. Prague is a place that leaves unforgettable memories and makes you want to return again.
